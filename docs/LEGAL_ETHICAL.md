# Legal and Ethical Notice

## Lawful Use Only
ForensiWipe is intended strictly for authorized and lawful data sanitization in controlled environments.

## Privacy and Data Destruction Implications
Secure deletion can permanently affect personal, institutional, or evidentiary records.  
Operators must verify ownership, permissions, and retention requirements before use.

## Digital Forensics and Evidence Handling
- Never use this tool on active legal evidence unless court/lab policy explicitly allows sanitization.
- Preserve chain-of-custody and evidentiary integrity before any destructive operation.
- Use Dry Run mode for classroom and demonstration contexts.

## Forensic Admissibility and Limitations
- Tool reports are educational and operational records, not automatic legal certification.
- Modern systems may retain residual traces through journaling, snapshots, SSD firmware behavior, and backups.

## Authorized Environment Requirement
Use only on systems and media where you have documented authorization from relevant owner/authority.
